Frequently Asked Questions

What macOS versions are supported?
SpeedOCR Studio requires macOS 14.0 or later on both Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs.
Does SpeedOCR Studio require internet access?
No. All OCR recognition runs 100% locally on your Mac using Apple's Vision framework and Neural Engine.
Where are my recordings and transcripts saved?
Every recording session creates a folder in ~/Movies/SpeedOCR-YYYYMMDD-HHMMSS/ containing capture.mp4, transcript.txt, ocr.srt, and ocr.jsonl.
